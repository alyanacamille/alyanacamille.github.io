Character: Adventurist, “mad” teacher, promotes “the real world”, name: Conrad Jason Setting: Future Utopia to dystopia, insane asylum Plot: realizes that the world he lives is a lie, (government is under a strict dictatorship), exposes the truth by teaching about “the real world”, then goes to insane asylum for “lying” about “the real world”, then escapes from asylum finding that the world has turned into a dystopia with a monster.

One day while teaching his 1st grade class that the world is flat, Conrad Jason has an awaking that what he is teaching is incorrect, as he goes on with his day he sees more and more the true colors of his Utopia society. Later on that day he goes into an antique store and finds a globe proving that the world is a sphere. He buys the globe in hope of exposing the truth to the youth of his society. The next day in classes he preaches of “the real world” to his students suddenly his is bombarded by state police, who then dramatically drag him out of his classroom into the street where his is publicly humiliated and called a liar and crazy. Then his is taken to an insane asylum, where he is put into solitary confinement for 16 years. Everyday his is fed one meal until one day he doesn't receive one, madden with hunger he tries to escape his confinement, and realises that the door to his cell is unlocked. He escapes the asylum into a dystopian society. Where he realizes that his is the last man on earth along with an mysterious monster. In fear disoriented Jason runs into a pole, either killing him or him waking up realizing it was just a dream.
